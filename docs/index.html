QC-GIS

Quad Cities GIS User Group

Establishing a GIS User Group in the QC area creates a network of partnerships with local organization, educational institutions and all other GIS users. It provides essential professional involvement in our local community, strengthens existing partnerships, and creates access to a pool of local GIS knowledge and resources.

Meetings

Meetings are held the 2 week of the month on a Tuesday or Friday from noon to 1pm at either Scott County EOC and Augustana College. Upcoming meeting info is emailed out to the user list ( please signup at the bottom of this page) prior to the meeting.
